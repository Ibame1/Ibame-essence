Our Purpose

Crafted with social utility.

IBAME operates as a social-economic business. We believe well-made goods should be part of your daily life. By actively engaging in a circular economy, we transform salvaged urban wood from Montréal, alongside Wenge, Dalbergia, and Iroko, into affordable, high-quality goods that serve a direct social purpose.
